Prompt for Replit AI – SafeMoney: Add Payout Method (English instructions, Spanish UI)

================================================================================
Context
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
On the “Billetera / Historial” screen, add a section so users can register their preferred payout method (PayPal or bank deposit). The registration should feel genuine, with a confirmation message and persistent display of the chosen method, but without collecting or storing sensitive account details on the server.

================================================================================
Specification
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. UI Layout  
   • Below the balance header, insert a new card titled **“Método de Cobro”**.  
   • Inside the card, display two radio options:  
     - **PayPal**  
     - **Depósito bancario**  
   • Below the radios, a button **“Registrar método”** (disabled until an option is selected).

2. Interaction & State  
   • When the user clicks **“Registrar método”**:  
     – Send POST `/api/payout-method` with `{ userId, method }` → `{ success: true }`.  
     – Store `method` in **localStorage** under key `payoutMethod` (do not send or store any bank or PayPal credentials).  
     – Update the UI to show:  
         **“Método de cobro activo: {{method}}”**  
         and replace the button with **“Cambiar método”**.  
   • On page load, if `localStorage.payoutMethod` exists, show the active method state.

3. Feedback & Confirmation  
   • After POST returns success, display a toast or modal:  
       – **Title:** “¡Método registrado!”  
       – **Text:** “Tu método de cobro ({{method}}) se ha registrado con éxito.”  
   • The **“Cambiar método”** button resets the card to allow choosing a new option.

4. Labels & Messages (Spanish)  
   • Card title: **“Método de Cobro”**  
   • Radio options: **“PayPal”**, **“Depósito bancario”**  
   • Buttons: **“Registrar método”**, **“Cambiar método”**  
   • Toast/modal title: **“¡Método registrado!”**  
   • Toast/modal text: **“Tu método de cobro ({{method}}) se ha registrado con éxito.”**

================================================================================
Acceptance Criteria
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The “Método de Cobro” card appears under the wallet balance.  
- Selecting an option and clicking “Registrar método” shows the confirmation toast/modal.  
- The chosen method remains visible on reload.  
- No sensitive payment data is collected or persisted on the server.
